**Pyramid** is a powerful economy and player retention plugin designed to drive server growth through viral recruitment mechanics. By incentivizing players to invite their friends and maintain active downlines, you can create a self-sustaining ecosystem of engagement and activity.

## Core Concepts

- **Viral Growth**: Players are financially rewarded for recruiting new members to the server.
- **Retention**: Automated activity checks ensure that rewards are only given to active participants, encouraging daily logins.
- **Economic Integration**: Seamlessly integrates with Vault to process transactions without performance overhead.
- **Multi-Tiered Revenue**: Creates a depth of engagement by rewarding players for the activity of their recruits' recruits.
- **Visual Dashboard**: A professional GUI allows players to manage their organization and track performance.
